More About

GCA as it is known as the best infrastructure in the city, equipped with four turf and 3 cement wickets. The academy has a total strength of 60 boys as we believe at the Academy under the able guidance of Madan Lal believe in quality coaching not quantity.

In order to give the players the enhance there skill even more we also have two bowling machines and video analysis sessions of individuals keep happening from time to time in order to teach the boys the finer points of the game. A lot of the boys from the academy have gone on to represent there states at junior level as well as first class level, and continue to bring laurels to the academy.
